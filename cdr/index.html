About CDR Analysis

What is CDR?

Call Data Records (CDR) contain metadata about phone calls including caller/callee numbers, timestamps, duration, and cell tower information.

Investigation Use

CDR analysis helps identify communication patterns, frequent contacts, location movements, and timeline of events in investigations.

Privacy Protected

All CDR analysis happens locally in your browser. No call records or personal data is uploaded to any server.
