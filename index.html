Legal Framework

Built in compliance with Indian digital evidence laws

BSA 2023 - Section 63

Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam, 2023 provides the framework for admissibility of electronic records as evidence. Section 63(4)(c) requires proper certification of digital evidence.

Certificate Generation

Our tools generate Part A and Part B certificates as required under Section 63(4) for proper documentation of electronic evidence authenticity.

Privacy First

All processing happens locally in your browser. No files are ever uploaded to any server, ensuring complete confidentiality of sensitive evidence.
